about my school

“A place to learn, a time to grow, education to last a lifetime!”

Glenview is a vibrant large senior school with over 700 students in Grades 7 and 8. We welcome students into our regular English program, French Immersion and Extended French. Students are involved in a wide-variety of activities such as sports, music, drama, arts-based programs and technology. We offer leadership opportunities to our students through our Student Leadership Council, Board initiatives such as Super Council and school-wide events.
